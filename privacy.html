Lux Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 16, 2026

Lux is a private family assistant app developed and operated by the Blair family for personal use. It is not available to the general public.

Data Collection

Lux collects voice and text input from authorized family members to provide assistant features. This data is processed on Blair-operated servers and is not shared with third parties.

Data Storage

Conversation history and personal preferences are stored on private servers controlled by the Blair family. No data is sold or shared with advertisers.

Contact

For questions, contact joel@blairforest.com.
